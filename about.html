Frequently Asked Questions

What is ArtisanHub?

ArtisanHub is a marketplace that connects customers with independent makers offering handmade and thoughtfully crafted products.

Are all products handmade?

Yes. Every product listed on ArtisanHub is created by a real artisan using traditional techniques and quality materials.

Where do your artisans come from?

Our artisans are based mainly in Ireland and across Europe, with a strong focus on local and small-scale makers.

How can I support an artisan?

You can support artisans by purchasing their products, sharing their work, and choosing handmade over mass-produced items.
